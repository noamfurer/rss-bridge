    The Yacht

    The centre cockpit of the yacht is extremely safe and you feel comfortable even in larger swells.

    The yacht is a so-called ketch, i.e. it has 2 masts and thus lies very stable in the water.

    The winches for operating the main sail and the genoa can be operated both electrically and manually from the cockpit.

    Only the smaller mizzen sail has to be operated by hand.

    The anchor winch is also electric and has a 100 m chain with a 30 kg anchor, which has never let us down so far.

    This winch can also be operated from the cockpit.

    There is another anchor at the stern of the yacht and one for the dinghy.

    All instruments are clearly visible and are located in the cockpit.

    In the stern there is a wind generator and 3 solar panels to generate further power, as well as a davit to which the dinghy can be attached.

    Under the seat on the starboard side are 2 scuba tanks and an air compressor for diving.

    Then we have two fishing rods to supply us with fresh fish.

    In the other boxes are small storm sails, a spare sail and a spinnaker.

    The yacht has 4 cabins and sleeps 6. The kitchen is fully equipped with cooker, refrigerator, freezer, microwave and sink. And all this with a headroom of 2.20 m, so that even tall people can work well in it.

    All cabins have air conditioning and hatches for light and ventilation.

    There is a 220 volt generator to charge the batteries and to run the washing machine.

    We also have a water maker that can produce 160 litres of fresh water per hour.
